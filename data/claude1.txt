Samsung unveiled its 2025 Bespoke appliance lineup featuring extensive AI integration. The centerpiece is a 4-door French-door refrigerator available with 9-inch ($3,999) or 32-inch ($4,699) displays, plus AI Vision Inside that recognizes 37 fresh ingredients and 50 processed foods. Other products include a hybrid fridge with Peltier cooling, AI-powered wall ovens with cameras, a quiet dishwasher at 38 decibels, a combo washer/dryer completing cycles in 68 minutes, and a powerful cordless vacuum with 400AW suction and 100-minute battery life.
